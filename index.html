Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with small to medium-sized businesses across various industries. Whether you're in retail, services, technology, or manufacturing, our frameworks and methodologies can be adapted to your specific needs. We've helped clients in e-commerce, professional services, franchises, and more.
How long does a typical engagement last?
Engagement length varies based on the service and your needs. A business launch consultation typically takes 4-8 weeks, acquisition analysis 2-4 weeks, and growth strategy development 6-12 weeks. We can also provide ongoing advisory support for implementation.
What's included in your pricing?
Our pricing is customized based on the scope of work. Typical packages include all analysis, templates, financial models, strategy documents, and a specified number of consultation hours. We'll provide a detailed proposal after our initial discovery call.
Do you provide ongoing support after the initial engagement?
Yes! We offer several ongoing support options including monthly advisory retainers, implementation support packages, and ad-hoc consulting. Many clients find value in having us as a strategic partner as they execute their plans.
What makes your approach different?
We combine Fortune 500 rigor with startup agility. You get enterprise-grade analysis and frameworks at accessible pricing. We focus on practical, actionable recommendations rather than lengthy reports that sit on shelves. Our founders have walked the path you're on and bring real-world experience to every engagement.
How do I get started?
Simply reach out via email or fill out our questionnaire. We'll discuss your situation, answer questions, and determine if we're a good fit. There's no obligation, and you'll leave with valuable insights regardless.
